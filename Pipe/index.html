[GAME]Fluid through a Pipe

Help

When water flows through a pipe, its velocity v will vary with the cross-sectional area A of the pipe so that the quantity Q=vA remains constant.

Play Around

Drag the red handles up and down to change the diameter of the pipe, and watch how the velocity of the fluid changes.

Play Game

When you click the "Play Game" button, a set of red lines will appear, which change speed as they move from left to right. Your goal is to adjust the size of the pipe so that the speed of the fluid matches the speed of the red lines. When you get it right, the word "Correct!" will appear.
