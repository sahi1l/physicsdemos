Lander

Newton's Laws say that an object will maintain a constant velocity unless acted upon by an outside force. This force will cause the ship to accelerate according to the formula a=F/m.

Instructions

  • Press the up key for thrust, left/right to rotate the ship
  • The ship will bounce off the walls; it won't crash.
  • The goal is to hit the yellow target with a speed less than 0.5 m/s.
  • There are two missions to complete, which you can select with the buttons labelled "Gravity" and "Deep Space" (aka "No Gravity"). You must complete both missions for full credit. When a mission is complete, its button will turn yellow.
  • You may restart a mission by pressing its button.
  • Numbers above the ship show the components of its velocity and acceleration.
