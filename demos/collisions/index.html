Elastic Collisions




  • Click on a number (mass or velocity) and drag left or right to change its value.
  • Press Start to launch the blocks.
  • Press Reset to return them to their initial positions.
  • Create the following scenarios. When they all turn red, the simulation is complete.
    • Initially the blocks move toward each other; after the collision they move in the same direction.
    • Initially the blocks move toward each other; after the collision they move in opposite directions.
    • Initially both blocks move in the same direction; after the collision they move in opposite directions.
    • Initially the right block is stationary; after the collision the left block is stationary. Hint: it doesn't matter what the initial velocity of the left block is; only the relative masses of the two blocks matter.
