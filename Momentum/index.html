[QUIZ]Inelastic Collisions

Goal: choose the velocity of the red ball so that, when it collides with the blue ball, the two come to a stop.

  • There are only internal forces when the two balls collide, so their total momentum remains constant.
  • The numbers on the balls indicate their masses; the numbers under the arrows are their speeds.
  • Drag the red slider backward to increase the speed of the red ball. When the velocity is correct, press the Launch button to start the balls moving.
  • Click the Again button for the next set of numbers.
